Understanding Your Rhythm 📖

A little guide to what your beautiful body is doing in each phase.

Rest and Recover (Days 1-5)
This is your period lovey. Your body is shedding its lining, and your hormones are at their lowest. It is completely normal if your cramps hurt or if you just want to sleep. I am here to get you hot compresses, feed you sweets, and just hold you while you rest.
Glowing (Days 6-14)
Your estrogen levels are rising, giving you a beautiful boost of natural energy. You might feel more focused and happy. It is the perfect time for us to go out on dates or for you to tackle those hard college assignments. You always look so radiant during this phase baby.
Peak Beauty (Days 15-17)
This is your ovulatory phase, my gorgeous girl. Your hormones are peaking, and you might feel extra confident, social, and energetic. This is also when your body naturally increases its libido, so it is the perfect time for us to be a little extra intimate and have some sexy time cutie.
Cuddle (Days 18-31)
As your body prepares for your next cycle, your hormones can become a bit unstable. Your energy starts to drop, and you might experience PMS, bloating, or mood swings. That is completely okay. Let us just stay in, watch movies, and take things slow. I am always here to listen and cuddle my sweetest.
